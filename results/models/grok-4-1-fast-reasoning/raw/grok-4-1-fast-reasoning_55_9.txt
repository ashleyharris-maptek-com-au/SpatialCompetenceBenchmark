The points are given in general position, meaning no four are cocircular and no three are collinear. To compute the Delaunay triangulation, I need to find the set of triangles where the circumcircle of each triangle contains no other points in its interior.

With 40 points, computing this manually is impractical without a computational geometry library. In practice, I would use scipy.spatial.Delaunay in Python. Here's the code I would run:
