In marching squares, this saddle configuration (opposite corners having the same value) is the classic "ambiguous" case. There are two valid ways to connect the edges:
1. Connect bottom-left with top-right (both 1) and bottom-right with top-left (both 2)
2. Connect bottom-left with top-left and bottom-right with top-right


This is "ambiguous".
