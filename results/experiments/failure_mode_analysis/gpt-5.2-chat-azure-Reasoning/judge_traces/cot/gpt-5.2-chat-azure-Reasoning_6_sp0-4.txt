**Classifying projection failures**

I'm looking into different subpasses and their classifications. For Subpass 0, there's an incorrect count by 1, which I see as a Near-Miss Edge Case. Subpass 1 involves a duplicate voxel with a single defect, also a Near-Miss. Subpasses 2 and 3 deal with XY and XZ projection holes, respectively, both indicating global integration failures but classified as Local-Only. Finally, Subpass 4 shows XZ projection holes and possibly massive overfill, still a Local-Only case. It seems there's no trivialization in this scenario.
